Research Interests

Non invertible symmetry in 2D gravity systerms

Ising coupled to 2D gravity stuff, was a rich playground to test the limits of non crital strings and push our understanding of two matrix models in a nice physical system. It has been shown that Ising coupled to gravity admits Kramers Wannier line at criticality as a non-invertible symmetry. Our aim is to study and analyse this system and the fate of non invertible symmetries when take non perturbative effects into account.

Semi-chiral matter in 2D-SUGRA

The rich playground of 𝒩=2 supergravity have been througly investigated through looking coupling of chiral matter field in superspace formalism. To a large extent via the magic of mirror symmtery, one can extend many of the physical reslts to twisted chiral sector as well. However, the coupling of semi-chiral matter fields have largely been unexplored, and our aim is to try and develop a roboust framework to study these systems.
